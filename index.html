Project Idea

Overview

An umbrella that can act like a self-driving car could be an effective solution to the inconvenience of travelling in rainy weather.

Motivation

Most people don't like travelling in the rain. Not only because of the cold and wet, but also because of the inconvenience of travelling. Umbrellas are essential for travelling in the rain, but they are very inconvenient to hold on to yourself. You also have to be aware of tree branches, pedestrians and their umbrellas on the road to avoid collisions.

Description

My project idea is a smart umbrella that can avoid obstacles like a self-driving car.

Firstly, it is very inconvenient to travel in the rain with one hand on the umbrella. So the umbrella needs to be able to hover right above a person's head and be able to recognise the user's position and keep track of it, so that it can follow the user at all times. My idea is that the user could carry a reader on his or her person that could be connected to the umbrella to identify the user's location. I think it would be possible to attach a drone like propeller to the umbrella so that it can stay aloft and adjust its height.

Secondly, we need to be aware of obstacles such as branches or buildings, as well as pedestrians and their umbrellas on the road. We sometimes need to adjust the height and position of the umbrella to avoid scrapes. I think it is possible to add a 360 degree camera and processor to the umbrella. The camera takes live footage and sends the data to the processor, which determines the obstacle and adjusts the height and position with the propeller. This would enable automatic obstacle avoidance.

Tools and Technologies

The canopy, propellers, cameras, sensors and processors are the main hardware tools. The key technology lies in the sensing technology of the surrounding information, the high sensitivity and high performance of the various sensors and information processing technology of artificial intelligence that replaces the five human senses.

Skills Required

The need for a system or program that allows the processor to automatically identify obstacles and avoid them is key to this project. I think it is very feasible to make a project, but it is still difficult to develop and mature.

Outcome

If this project is successful and mature, it will greatly facilitate our travel in the rain. However, I think that the price may affect the demand for this project, so it will be a long time before this project can really happen.
